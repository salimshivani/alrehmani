ABOUT US

Al-Rehmani Sea Food is based in India headquartered in the coastal city of Porbandar (the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi) and branches in Okha & Dwarka, Gujarat. We are one of the largest fresh & live sea food exporter and the most trusted source of sea food knowledge and innovation in the seafood industry.
